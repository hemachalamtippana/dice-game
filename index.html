Rules Of Games


It's a very simple game, player 1 will start the game by clicking on the button "rolling dice". You will get chance to roll the dice as many times it doesn't show 1. As soon as 1 comes on the dice of 1st player , its chance for the second player and Vice-Versa. Number 1 on the dice will make you score zero. In between your chance you can hold your score, if you feel that the next dice will be 1. The 1st reach to 50 will win the game. I hope you enjoy the game. :)
